Directions
From Boston, MA and areas south:

Take US-1 North. From US-1 North merge onto I-95 North. From I-95 North take the US-4 / RT-16 exit, exit 4, on the LEFT toward N.H. Lakes / White Mts. / Spaulding Turnpike. Merge onto NH-16 North / Spaulding Turnpike. From NH-16 North take exit 16. Stay to your LEFT to take the Chestnut Hill Rd. exit. Take LEFT at the bottom of the exit. Turn LEFT onto Chestnut Hill Rd. Follow Chestnut Hill Rd. to a set of lights. Take RIGHT at lights onto Wakefield St. Spaulding High School is on the RIGHT.

From Lebanon, NH and areas west:

Take US-4 East / NH-120 S toward Hanover St. Turn LEFT onto US-4 E / South Park Street / NH-120 S. Continue to follow NH-120 S. Turn SLIGHT RIGHT onto US-4 W / NH-120 N / Hanover St. Continue to follow NH-120 N / Hanover St. Turn RIGHT onto NH-120. Merge onto I-89 S. Merge onto I-93 N toward I-393 / Concord / US-4 / Seacoast. Merge onto US-202 E / US-4 E via EXIT 15E toward Loudon / Portsmouth. Enter next roundabout and take 2nd exit onto US-4 E / US-202 E / NH-9 E. Turn LEFT onto US-202 / NH-9 / Rochester Rd. Continue to follow US-202. US-202 becomes Washington St. Take a RIGHT at the end of Washington St. Follow North Main St. through one set of lights. Move into left lane and merge left onto Wakefield St. Move into right lane to continue on Wakefield St. Spaulding High School is on your LEFT.

From Berlin, NH and areas north:

Take NH-16 S. Take the US-202 E / RT-11 E exit, EXIT 16, toward RT-125 / Rochester / Sanford, ME. Turn RIGHT. Turn LEFT onto Chestnut Hill Rd. Turn LEFT to stay on Chestnut Hill Rd. Turn RIGHT onto NH-125 / Wakefield St. End at Spauldin High School.

From Portland, ME and areas north-east:

Take I-295 S toward South Portland. Merge onto I-95 S. Take the US-4 / RT-16 exit, EXIT 5, toward US-1 BYP / Portsmouth / Newington. Merge onto NH-16 N / Spaulding Turnpike. From NH-16 North take exit 16. Stay to your LEFT to take the Chestnut Hill Rd. exit. Take LEFT at the bottom of the exit. Turn LEFT onto Chestnut Hill Rd. Follow Chestnut Hill Rd. to a set of lights. Take RIGHT at lights onto Wakefield St. Spaulding High School is on the RIGHT.
